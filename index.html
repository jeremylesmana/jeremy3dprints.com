Why order 3D Prints?

Here are a few reasons as to why you would want to or should order 3D Prints from me.

Replacement Parts

If you have broken a part from an object, tool, device, furniture, and more but you can't find the exact replacement; I can help you draft and print out a replacement part that you won't be able to find anywhere else in stores.

Hobby & Models

If you want your favorite characters or objects from movies, games, TV shows, books, novels, and videos come to life as a real-life physical model, I can do that for you!

Prototyping

If you want to make the next big invention but just need a little help prototyping your product and be able to physically hold it and experiment with sizing, designs, functionality, and etc, I can do that for you as well!

Gifts

With the 3D printer and the help of drafting, the possibilities are endless. You can customize many different things for someone, such as a customizable name tag, model, figure, and more!

About Me

Hi, my name is Jeremy and I am a computer science student attending Cal Poly Pomona. I enjoy drafting and 3D modeling on the side, and have always wanted to 3D print my own creations with other big companies, when I realized that they are charging me crazy prices from simple prints! Now that I am able to afford a 3D printer and learn the skills and crafts of handling one (temperatures, bed leveling, part replacement, adhesion, slicing, etc), I would want to give back to the community and charge super affordable pricing for 3D printing.
Never hesitate to contact me regarding an order before you place one, I am always happy to help you and talk with you to ensure the best outcome for your project!
